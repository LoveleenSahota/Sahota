2️⃣ Core Financial Statements to Analyze
🧾 1. Income Statement (P&L)

Focus:

Revenue growth

Gross margin

Operating expenses

EBITDA

Net profit

Real-time decision examples:

Revenue slowing? → Check churn & new bookings

Marketing cost rising? → Check CAC efficiency

Gross margin dropping? → Infrastructure cost issue?

🏦 2. Balance Sheet

Focus:

Cash position

Accounts Receivable

Deferred revenue

Liabilities

Real-time decisions:

AR increasing? → Tighten dunning

Deferred revenue growing? → Strong future revenue base

Cash runway dropping? → Cut burn rate

💰 3. Cash Flow Statement

Focus:

Operating cash flow

Free cash flow

Burn rate

Real-time decisions:

Positive P&L but negative cash? → AR collection issue

High prepaid expenses? → Over-investment risk
